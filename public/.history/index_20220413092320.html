Everybody needs a lil art in their life.

Hey! My name is Ibtihel Ben Salah, and I'm an artist based in hammam lif. If you're looking for some new art to liven up your living space or office, I can help you out! Whether you're looking for moody vibes or bright colors, I've got something that will catch your eye.

My pieces come in a variety of sizes and styles, so they're perfect for any space. They're also all made with professional-quality materials and techniques—so these aren't just some paintings your buddy's kid did on the weekend. You can feel confident knowing that your walls are decorated with original artwork.

I've got a gallery of my work, but if you'd like to see my work in person before you buy, shoot me an email and we'll set up a time to chat—or even just get coffee!
